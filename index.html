New Instruments

For the Reconquest of Practical Knowledge

Convivial Tools are transducers
or amplifiers of energy.

They allow us to
enrich our environment
with the fruits of our vision.

They can be easily used
by anybody,
as often
or
as seldom
as desired,
for a purpose
chosen by the user.

Their existence does not impose
any obligation to use them.

They allow the user to
express their meaning in action.

Steven di Blasio is a Convivialist Product Designer living in Portland, Oregon. He has worked in lead roles at a number of now-defunct Silicon Valley startups and in advisory roles at a number of others. He can be reached here via email.
